Volume - Persistent Volume Claim

k8s/devopstree-pvc.yml


apiVersion: v1
kind: PersistentVolumeClaim
metadata:
  name: db-pvc
  labels:
    type: local
spec:
  accessModes:
    - ReadWriteOnce
  resources:
    requests:
      storage: 2Gi
  volumeName: db-pv
  storageClassName: standard

Create volume claim:


$ kubectl apply -f ./k8s/devopstree-pvc.yml
View details: 

$ kubectl get pvc
