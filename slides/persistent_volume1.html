Volume - Persistent Volume


  apiVersion: v1
kind: PersistentVolume
metadata:
  name: db-pv
  labels:
    type: local
spec:
  capacity:
    storage: 2Gi
  storageClassName: standard
  accessModes:
    - ReadWriteOnce
  hostPath:
    path: "/data/db-pv"

Create the volume:


$ kubectl apply -f ./k8s/devopstree-pv.yml
View details: 

$ kubectl get pods
