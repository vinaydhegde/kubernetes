Kubernetes Concepts (continued...)

  • Volumes

    • Volumes are used to persist data beyond the life of a container. They are especially important for stateful applications like Redis and Postgres.

    1. PersistentVolume defines a storage volume independent of the normal Pod-lifecycle. It's managed outside of the particular Pod that it resides in.
    2. PersistentVolumeClaim is a request to use the PersistentVolume by a user.
